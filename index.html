Why Choose Aluminum Gates?

Cost

Aluminum gates are less costly than steel gates. 10 years ago they were alot more expensive than Steel gates.

Weight

Aluminum custom made gates are very light compare to Steel gates, making them a lot easier to open compared to those made of iron.

Durability

Since aluminum is not damaged by rust or corrosion, your gate can serve you well for many years.

Appearance

When talking about sheer beauty, our aluminum gates are so elegant because the materials that we use in most cases are TWICE as thick than a metal gate.

Maintenance

Maintenance is out of the question. Aluminum is the way to go, since it does not corrode, unlike with wrought Iron (steel), you are not required to perfom maintenance to up keep your gate.
